# Dhundra
A self hosted resume tailoring tool and tracker for your job hunt journey.

## What does this tool do?

- Tailor your resume according to a job description.  
- Check if your resume is relevant to a job description.  
- Generate cover letters.  
- Track the resumes you generated.  
- A heatmap to help you stay consistent.

---

## Why did I make this?

- I wanted to improve my chances of clearing the ATS filtration process.  
- Using 1 resume everywhere wouldn’t be the best way to get selected.  
- Online AI tools felt expensive. (I didn’t want to pay $5-10 a week.)  
- GPT4o-mini API is super cheap in comparison.  
- I like building side projects that solve a problem.
